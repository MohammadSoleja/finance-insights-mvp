Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.

Is there a free trial?

The Professional plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), debit cards, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and in transit. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes! We offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for details.
